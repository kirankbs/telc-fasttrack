## SSML calibration notes for render pass

- All files use `rate="1.0"` — B2 natural speed, no accommodation.
- Opening `<break time="10s"/>` before the section instruction matches the pre-audio buffer used in mock_03 reference. At render time, if the TTS pipeline prepends its own silence this can be reduced to `3s`.
- Inter-item breaks: `5s` between Teil 1 and Teil 3 items; `1s` turn-taking pauses in Teil 2 interviews. The `1s` pauses feel tight on first listen — consider bumping to `1.5s` for the interview turns if the rendered output sounds rushed.
- Teil 2 male moderator voice is `de-DE-Wavenet-E` in mocks 07–10. This is consistent with mock_03 where `E` was used for the male moderator role. Do not swap to `B` — `B` is reserved for news readers in Teil 1/3.
- Mock 09 Teil 2 guest (Dr. Julia Mertens) and mock 10 Teil 2 guest (Dr. Judith Mertens) both use `de-DE-Wavenet-A`. Same voice, different character names — intentional per the Wavenet assignment spec where `A` is the primary female dialogue voice.
- No repeat markers: all B2 parts play ONCE (`playCount=1`).
